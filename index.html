  About me

Occupation.    I am currently an IMPRS-IS & ELLIS PhD Student at the University of Tuebingen, co-supervised by Zeynep Akata (University of Tuebingen) and Oriol Vinyals (Google Deepmind, London).

Research.    My current research focuses on effective multimodal, contrastive and metric representation learning, with a focus of developing system capable of strong Out-of-Distribution generalization.

Previous.    I was lucky enough to have worked with amazing people at Amazon AWS Tuebingen (Peter Gehler, Thomas Brox), Vector Institute Toronto (Marzyeh Ghassemi) and MILA Montreal (Joseph Paul Cohen, Yoshua Bengio).

Pre-ML I completed my Master and Bachelor of Physics at Heidelberg University with initial focus on Medical and Solid State Physics.

Reviewer Duties    for MICCAI, IJCAI, MIDL, AutoML, wNeurIPS, CVPR, ECCV
